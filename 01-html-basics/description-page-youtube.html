YouTube is a massive, Google-owned online video-sharing platform where users can upload, watch, share, and comment on videos, becoming the world's second-most-visited website and a hub for entertainment, education, news, and a thriving creator economy, allowing individuals to build careers and businesses through content creation, from vlogs and tutorials to music and films. Founded in 2005, it hosts billions of videos and serves billions of users, offering diverse content formats, monetization options, and services like YouTube Music, YouTube TV, and YouTube Premium.
